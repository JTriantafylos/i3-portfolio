Computer Science | Grade 11

### My experiences in ICS3U1, the grade 11 comp-sci course at Sacred Heart CHS.

Experience

### By taking this grade 11 computer science course I was able to expand my knowledge of web development greatly and ultimately be capable of creating this very portfolio website.


### While to my dismay we did not learn very much in terms of writing code, we did learn how to effectively create visually appealing and functional webpages and with the help of sites such as Stack Overflow I was able to expand my knowledge around the Javascript portion of web development to accomplish some, in my opinion, interesting tasks.


Outcome

### Overall I found this course to not only teach me the in's and out's of web development but to also respark my love for coding through the many different styles of projects we were required to do.


### It is easy to get overwhelmed by working on multiple coding projects at once and end up quitting all of them all together due to not knowing where to go next, but having the structure of a course based around computer science allowed me to better prioritize my time and to be more organized when it came to personal project and school projects.


### Ultimately this course was a pleasure for me to be involved in and I will surely take the grade 12 computer science course to further build on all that I learned this year.
